# Related Work

Concepts that surround the AX Stack but live as standalone work.

## Context Sovereignty

**Question:** Who controls the canonical version of your context?
**Layer:** Context (★).
**Status:** active research, no widely-adopted answer.
**Why it matters:** Without sovereignty, the vendor's vault is the canonical version. The user is renting their own perspective.

## Temporal UX

**Question:** Coordination problems across Human→Agent, Agent→Agent, Network→Network axes.
**Layer:** Orchestration + Accountability.
**Why it matters:** Agents act in milliseconds; humans observe in minutes; networks reconcile in hours. The gaps between these timescales produce silent failures.

## Killerjourney

**Question:** Apps are doors, not destinations; agents change who walks through them.
**Layer:** Cross-cutting (Intent + Orchestration + Relationship).
**Why it matters:** The product question is no longer "what does the user do here?" but "whose moment is this — and is it the human's or the agent's?"
